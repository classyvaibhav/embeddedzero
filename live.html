Live study session

This page sends your microphone audio to your tutor in real time so he can listen while you read aloud and step in if you get stuck. Click Start session when you sit down to study, and End session when you're done.

When you click Start, your browser will ask permission to use the microphone — say Allow once. While the session is live you'll see a red dot in this tab and a microphone icon in your browser's address bar. Hit End session any time to stop.
