How to Use This Guide

Daily Flow

  1. Pre-session (5 min): Open the relevant topic card. Read the Gotcha and Check sections so you know where she'll stumble.
  2. Run the session (45 min): Use the suggested script. Follow the 45-min template from the Overview page.
  3. Assign the lab: Link from the topic card to the corresponding Lab entry. Give her a deadline (next session).
  4. Post-session: Note what check-questions she missed. Hit those in next session's 5-min recap.

Key Design Choices in This Guide

  • SVG-first diagrams. Every schematic in this guide is inline SVG — no broken images, no external dependencies. Just open the file.
  • Answers embedded in check-questions. You don't have to remember or look up — the answer is right there in a monospace tag.
  • Gotcha boxes are first-class. Every topic has one, because teaching means pre-empting confusion, not reacting to it.
  • Application-level only. No RTOS, no bare-metal C, no deep firmware. She needs Arduino-level confidence first.
  • Symbol references are visual. The Symbols page has SVG graphics for every component — not just a name-table.
