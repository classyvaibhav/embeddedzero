Lab Philosophy

Every concept gets a hands-on session within 24 hours of theory. Labs use the ESP32 kit (~₹1800) from Overview page. If hardware delayed, use Tinkercad Circuits or Wokwi simulator — both free, browser-based, fine for first exposure.
